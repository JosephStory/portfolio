005 : Tun Abdul Razak Research Centre

A summer internship working on a tyre testing trailer at TARRC proved to be an invaluable experience. I designed a custom Anti-Lock Braking System, writing the code in C++ whilst also producing the electrical and mechanical components. Furthermore, I finished off a pneumatic system to adjust the toe-in and toe-out angle of the tyres. As an additional project, I utilised CAD to 3D print components for a multi-axial tyre testing rig.
